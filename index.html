Introduction to Web Development

Web development is the process of creating websites and web applications. It encompasses various aspects including:

Front-End Development

Front-end development focuses on what users interact with in a web browser. It involves:

  • HTML (HyperText Markup Language) - structure and content
  • CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) - design and layout
  • JavaScript - adding interactivity and functionality

Back-End Development

Back-end development powers the functionalities behind the scenes. It involves:

  • Server-side scripting languages like PHP, Python, Ruby, etc.
  • Database systems (e.g., MySQL, MongoDB) for data storage
  • Frameworks and libraries for building and managing applications

Full-Stack Development

Full-stack developers work on both the front-end and back-end, handling the entire web development process.

Responsive Design and Accessibility

Developers aim for responsive design to ensure websites work well on various devices. Additionally, accessibility focuses on making websites usable for all users, including those with disabilities.

Binary Challenge

Steps to Convert Decimal to Binary:

  1. Repeated Division: Start by dividing the number by 2 and noting the remainder.
  2. Repeat the Division: Continue dividing the quotient by 2 until the quotient becomes 0.
  3. Read the Remainders: The remainders will provide the binary number in reverse order.

Example: Convert the decimal number 23 to binary:

  1. Step 1: Divide 23 by 2. The quotient is 11, and the remainder is 1. (23 ÷ 2 = 11 remainder 1)
  2. Step 2: Divide 11 by 2. The quotient is 5, and the remainder is 1. (11 ÷ 2 = 5 remainder 1)
  3. Step 3: Divide 5 by 2. The quotient is 2, and the remainder is 1. (5 ÷ 2 = 2 remainder 1)
  4. Step 4: Divide 2 by 2. The quotient is 1, and the remainder is 0. (2 ÷ 2 = 1 remainder 0)
  5. Step 5: Divide 1 by 2. The quotient is 0, and the remainder is 1. (1 ÷ 2 = 0 remainder 1)
